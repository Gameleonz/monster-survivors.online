About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating hordes of enemies.

As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and characters, each with unique strengths and playstyles. The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two playthroughs are exactly the same.

With its addictive gameplay loop and simple controls, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive the monster onslaught?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Press Space to use special ability (when available)
  • Press P to pause the game
  • On mobile devices, use the virtual joystick to move

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Collect experience gems to level up and gain new abilities
  • Prioritize health upgrades early in your run
  • Different weapons work better against different enemy types
  • Try different character builds to find your optimal playstyle

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, Monster Survivors is completely free to play online. No downloads or installations required.

Absolutely! The game is fully optimized for both desktop and mobile play with touch controls.

Yes, your game progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. For the best experience, avoid clearing your browser data.
