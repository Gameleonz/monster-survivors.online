**About The Game**

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game where your combat skills and strategy will be pushed to the limit. As waves of monsters grow stronger and more numerous, you must adapt your tactics and upgrade your abilities to withstand the onslaught.

Set in a world overrun by diverse creatures, you'll navigate through different environments, collecting power-ups and experience points to evolve your character. Each run presents unique challenges as you aim to survive longer than your previous attempts.

The game features multiple character classes, each with distinctive abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer ranged attacks, close combat, or magical abilities, Monster Survivors offers gameplay variety that keeps you coming back for more.

**How to Play**

**Controls**

Master these controls to navigate through hordes of monsters:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Mouse Aim your attacks or abilities
  • Space Activate special abilities when available
  • E Interact with objects or collect items

**Basic Gameplay**

Your goal is simple but challenging: survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience points to level up and unlock powerful upgrades for your character.

The game continues until your health points reach zero, so managing your resources and choosing the right upgrades is crucial for extending your survival time.

**Strategy Tips**

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Focus on a consistent build strategy when selecting upgrades
  • Prioritize health recovery items when your HP is low
  • Learn the attack patterns of different monster types
  • Use environmental features to your advantage when possible
