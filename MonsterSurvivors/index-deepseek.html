About the Game

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game combining roguelite mechanics with fast-paced combat. Face endless waves of unique monsters, collect powerful upgrades, and discover hidden synergies to extend your survival time in this procedurally generated nightmare realm.

Key Features

  • Dynamic procedural level generation
  • 30+ unique monster types
  • RPG-style character progression
  • Online leaderboards

Gameplay Tips

  • Combine abilities for powerful synergies
  • Prioritize mobility upgrades early
  • Learn monster attack patterns
  • Experiment with different builds

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, the game is completely free to play with optional cosmetic upgrades.

What platforms are supported?

The game runs directly in your browser and supports both PC and mobile devices.
