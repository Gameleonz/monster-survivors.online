Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and discover powerful synergies to overcome the challenge.

With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of fun as you try to beat your high score and unlock new characters and abilities.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Collect experience gems to level up. When you level up, you can choose from a selection of upgrades and new abilities.

Survive as long as possible and defeat as many monsters as you can to achieve a high score!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Unlock and play as different characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles.

Upgrade System

Level up and choose from powerful upgrades to create your own unique build strategy.

Epic Abilities

Unleash devastating attacks and defensive abilities to overcome the monster hordes.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement speed early to better avoid enemy attacks
  • Don't neglect defensive abilities - they become crucial in later waves
  • Try to create synergies between your abilities for maximum effectiveness
  • Prioritize collecting experience gems to level up faster
  • Some rare abilities might seem weak at first but can become extremely powerful when upgraded
